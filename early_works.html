Early Works

Island and Peninsula Series

This series emerged out of my interest in the natural and human-made landmarks that are found on islands and peninsulas around the world. I gathered an assortment of papers and sketched the desired shapes with tracing paper. Then, I transferred the outlines from the tracing paper onto the other papers and cut them out. After making these clippings, I drew on them and applied color before piecing
them together onto a larger piece of paper.
