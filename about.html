Biography

Brendan Reynolds Stoianov is native to Columbia, South Carolina.
His background in visual art originates from a childhood fascination with
two- and three-dimensional forms. During his education as a Bachelor of
Fine Arts major at the University of South Carolina, Brendan’s work has
changed from featuring representational to non-representational imagery.
Since graduating with his BFA in May 2025, he has gone on to pursue a
Master of Architecture degree. His work currently explores how transform-
ation is central to processes in nature. He strives to incorporate this theme
into his designs for the natural and the built environment.
