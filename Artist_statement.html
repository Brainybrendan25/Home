Artist Statement

My current artwork is based on the theme of transformation since “to transform” can mean many different things.
I present enough ambiguity that makes viewers question what they're seeing. Since I am interested in exploring
how transformation occurs across dimension, I use anaglyphs — overlapping red and cyan outlines that give the
illusion of 3D form — as a platform to investigate this phenomenon. While my past works are entirely 2D, the
anaglyphs in my current work require that each piece be viewed through 3D glasses. My work evokes different
feelings through variations in line weight and line type. Most of the forms that I gravitate towards tend to undulate
in some way. These kinds of marks best capture transformation occurring in some shape or form.
