Home

I draw with a diverse range of media at various scales to create my body of work. In the past,
I have used more traditional media like acrylic paint, colored pencils, gouache, graphite pencils, ink,
ink pens, sharpie and watercolor. At present, I have worked with more unconventional materials like
digital prints and food coloring. Whatever the material choice, my theme has stayed the same, which
is to depict transformation occurring as a dimensional change.
